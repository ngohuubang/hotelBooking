Welcome to VietNam The Beautiful Country of the Whole World

Vietnam, officially the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, is a country located at the easternmost tip of the Indochinese peninsula in Southeast Asia, bordering Laos, Cambodia, China, the East Sea and the Gulf Thailand.
The territory of Vietnam has been inhabited since the Paleolithic era. Ancient Vietnamese history began with the states of Van Lang and Au Lac. Au Lac was annexed by the Trieu Dynasty from the North in the early 2nd century BC; followed by a period of Northern domination that lasted more than a millennium. The independent monarchy was re-established after Ngo Quyen's victory over the Southern Han Dynasty.
